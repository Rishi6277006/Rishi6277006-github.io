About Me

Data Scientist & Machine Learning Engineer

Hello! I'm Rishikesh Thakker, a passionate Data Scientist and Machine Learning Engineer with expertise in building AI-driven solutions for real-world problems. I'm currently pursuing my Master's in Applied Data Science at Syracuse University, where I'm enhancing my skills in machine learning, natural language processing, and cloud computing.

My journey in technology began with a Bachelor's degree in Electronics and Telecommunication, where I developed a strong foundation in mathematics and programming. This background has been invaluable in my transition to data science, providing me with a unique perspective on solving complex problems.

I specialize in developing machine learning models for predictive analytics and automation. I've worked on various projects, from vacancy prediction using Random Forest models to building NLP pipelines for analyzing political debates. I'm particularly interested in the intersection of machine learning and cloud computing, developing scalable solutions that can generate actionable insights from large datasets.

I'm constantly learning and expanding my skill set to stay at the forefront of data science and AI advancements. I believe in the power of data to transform businesses and solve important challenges across industries.
